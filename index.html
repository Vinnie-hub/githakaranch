Who We Are

Built on trust and good stock.

Githaka Ranch sits on the Nyeri–Nanyuki Highway in Narumoru. We breed Sahiwal cattle and supply farm produce, dairy and equipment. Every animal is checked and ready before it leaves our gate.

  • 100+ heifers, bulls and calves in stock now
  • All animals vaccinated and dewormed before sale
  • Farm visit with lunch and training — KSh 2,800
  • Tours every Thursday and Saturday, by appointment
  • Visit fee (KSh 2,800) refunded if you purchase livestock that day
  • Delivery nationwide, with vet help after your purchase
